What is Hinavah?

Hinavah is a traditional Kadazan-Dusun dish made from raw fish marinated with lime juice, ginger, onions, and other ingredients. Unlike cooked fish, Hinavah is prepared without heat. Instead, acid is used to alter the protein structure.

What is Denaturation?

Protein denaturation refers to changes in the three-dimensional structure of proteins without breaking peptide bonds.
